In short, Flyt™ uses satellite information from Copernicus, combined with additional data, to inform professional truck drivers on the Norwegian road network about potential traffic jams.

This is very useful for the freight transport business, especially in the big cities, and around holiday seasons, when the Norwegian traffic network gets completely bottlenecked.

Every other day, traffic jams can develop in a very short time, and this is when Flyts™ unique process of finding other more suitable roads for the drivers kicks inn.

In addition to shortening the delivery time, our solution also helps shorten traffic jams all around the country, this by automatically routing truck drivers on to alternative roads.

Forget about Google Maps, TomTom or any other random GPS, if you are a truck driver in Norway, Flyt™ is definitely your best solution!
